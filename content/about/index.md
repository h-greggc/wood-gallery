## Do-It-Yourself Genetics!

I have been both blessed and cursed by DIY genetics! My parents, grandparents, uncles and aunts were all do-it-yourself (DIY) folks. Not on the typical minor remodeling, but many of them had built or participated in building their homes. That's a whole lot of learning opportunity.

My beginning instruction in woodworking included a couple of semesters in junior high and high school; however, most of my first lessons were with my father, my maternal grandfather--who had introduced my father to the craft--or some paternal-side uncles. These first-person learning opportunities led me to a lifetime interest, and occasionally, even an intense hobby!
